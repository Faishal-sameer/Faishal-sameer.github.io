Strategic Merger Analysis in the OTT Domain

Mar 2026
Advanced SQL Business Case Study Market Intelligence

The Business Problem: In India’s highly competitive streaming landscape, platforms struggle with converting mass user acquisition into sustainable revenue, facing high user churn rates and low paid-tier conversion.

Analyzed a prospective strategic merger between LioCinema and Jotstar using comprehensive SQL relational querying to model mass scale vs active premium monetization dynamics.

Key Insights Extracted:
  • Multiplier Effect: LioCinema drives high volume top-of-funnel mass user acquisition while Jotstar drives platform engagement and higher monetization.
  • Churn Risk: Low watch-time users carry the highest churn propensity. Tier 2/3 cities represent high active user growth, whereas Tier 1 drives consistent premium margins.
Strategic Recommendations: Implement step-based tiered pricing models to transition free tiers, launch highly localized affordable plans for regional markets, and employ targeted re-engagement notification pipelines to minimize churn.
